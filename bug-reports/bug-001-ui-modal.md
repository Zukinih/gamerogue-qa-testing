Title: Dice roll modal does not close after action

Environment:
- Platform: Web
- Browser: Chrome
- OS: Windows 10

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Join a game session
2. Open the dice roll modal
3. Roll the dice
4. Click outside the modal

Expected Result:
The modal should close when clicking outside of it.

Actual Result:
The modal remains open and blocks interaction with the UI.

Severity:
Medium

Notes:
This issue impacts user flow during gameplay and may cause frustration
during fast-paced sessions.
